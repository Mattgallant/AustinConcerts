Kesha

Bio:
Kesha Rose Sebert ( born March 1, 1987), known mononymously as Kesha (formerly stylized Ke$ha), is an American singer, songwriter and rapper. In 2005, at age 18, Kesha was signed to Kemosabe Records. Her first major success came in early 2009 after she was featured on American rapper Flo Rida's number-one single "Right Round".
